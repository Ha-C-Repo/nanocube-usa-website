The difference

Not a pre-engineered metal building.

We frame in conventional rolled steel, not prefab kits. Your building carries the future loads, retrofits, and hanging equipment a PEMB cannot. A real structure, closed in tight, built to endure.

Where we fit your process

Five phases. One team.

01

Feasibility

We price the steel and envelope at massing, so the structure fits the pro forma before schematic design locks.

02

Coordination

In-house Tekla detailing that coordinates with your BIM model. Clash-checked shop drawings.

03

Engineering

Structural drawings sealed by a PE licensed in the project jurisdiction. Connections by delegated design.

04

Fabrication

AISC-compliant shop. Conventional rolled beams, columns, joists, and connections. QC-gated before steel leaves the floor.

05

Envelope + Erect

IMPs, insulation, waterproofing, soffits, and gutters, then field erection by Nano Cube ironworker crews.

What's included

The complete shell, itemized.

Structure
  • Conventional steel frame: W-shapes, HSS, channels, angles
  • Steel roof framing and open-web joists
  • Steel roof deck or standing-seam roof system
  • Opening framing, lintels, and lateral bracing
  • Miscellaneous metals: stairs, rails, supports
  • Equipment support platforms
  • Anchor rods and base plates
Envelope
  • Insulated metal panels (IMPs), roof and walls
  • Exterior aluminum panel systems
  • Continuous insulation and vapor control
  • Waterproofing and flashings
  • Soffits
  • Gutters and downspouts
  • Wall girts, wind columns, eave and base supports
