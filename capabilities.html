Our core capabilities

Four capabilities. One coordinated team.

Design, engineering, fabrication, erection, and the complete building shell, all in-house.

From design and engineering through fabrication, erection, and the complete building shell, we carry the full scope in-house. One company on the hook from the first calculation to a closed-in building.

We deliver conventional hot-rolled structural steel. Cold-formed and light-gauge steel framing (LGS) is excluded and coordinated by others.

/ 01

Structural Steel Design & Engineering

  • Structural analysis and design to AISC 360, IBC, and applicable local codes
  • Design development from concept, massing, or full CD sets
  • Engineered solutions for buildings, mezzanines, canopies, platforms, stairs, and special structures
  • SJI-compliant joist and joist-girder design, engineer-reviewed
  • PE-signed and stamped calculations and drawings
  • Value engineering of member sizes, connections, and overall steel tonnage
/ 02

Steel Fabrication

  • In-house fabrication of columns, beams, girders, joists, and bracing
  • HSS, W-shapes, plates, and built-up members
  • Stairs, handrails, guardrails, ladders, platforms, embeds, canopies, and architectural steel components
  • AWS D1.1 welding procedures
  • CNC cutting, drilling, and precision fit-up
  • AISC 207-25 shop standards with quality control at every stage

/ 03

Steel Erection

  • Professional erection crews for commercial, industrial, and multi-level structures
  • Detailed erection plans, sequencing, and crane coordination
  • Safety-focused execution to OSHA 1926
  • Roof deck and metal deck installation in scope
/ 04

Complete Building Shell / Envelope

  • Wall systems, roofing, and the full exterior envelope coordinated with the steel frame
  • Structural frame and building shell delivered as one package, one subcontract
  • Sequenced so the frame and envelope close in without gaps between trades
  • Continuous insulated envelope supporting energy targets and AIA 2030 goals
