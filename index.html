Our core capabilities

Five capabilities. One coordinated team.

From engineering and fabrication to erection and complete metal buildings, we carry the full scope in-house. One company on the hook from the first calculation to the last bolt.

/ 01

Structural Steel Engineering

  • Structural analysis and design to AISC, IBC, and applicable local codes
  • Engineered solutions for buildings, mezzanines, canopies, platforms, and special structures
  • SJI-compliant joist and joist-girder design, engineer-reviewed
  • PE-signed and stamped calculations and drawings
  • Value engineering of member sizes, connections, and overall steel tonnage
/ 02

Steel Fabrication

  • In-house fabrication of columns, beams, girders, joists, and bracing
  • HSS, W-shapes, plates, and built-up members
  • AWS-compliant welding procedures
  • CNC cutting, drilling, and precision fit-up
  • Quality control and inspection at every stage
/ 03

Steel Erection

  • Professional erection crews experienced in commercial and industrial work
  • Multi-level structures and mezzanines
  • Detailed erection plans, sequencing, and crane coordination
  • Safety-focused execution with adherence to OSHA requirements
/ 04

Misc. & Secondary Steel

  • Stairs, handrails, and guardrails
  • Ladders, platforms, frames, embeds, and custom steel assemblies
  • Canopies, supports, and architectural steel components
  • Fully coordinated shop drawings and connection details
/ 05

Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings (PEMB)

  • Complete PEMB manufacturing and delivery
  • Turnkey primary frames
  • Secondary framing: purlins, girts, and bracing
  • Roof and wall panels with insulation
  • Openings, trims, and accessories
  • Integrated engineering, fabrication, and erection for seamless delivery
