The difference

Not a pre-engineeredmetal building.

PEMBs lock you into a manufacturer's catalog, fixed bay spacing, and a thin-gauge frame. We build with conventional rolled structural steel. Custom geometry, real load capacity, and a building that reads as architecture, not a warehouse kit.

What a complete shell includes

Structure and envelope.

A finished shell is more than the frame. We carry the structure and the envelope together, so there is no gap between trades and no finger-pointing when it is time to close in.

Structure

  • Rolled structural steel columns and beams
  • Bar joists and joist girders
  • Moment frames and bracing
  • Base plates, anchor bolts, embeds
  • Connection design and detailing

Envelope

  • Roof deck, supply and installation
  • Composite and form deck
  • Wall and facade support steel
  • Canopies, soffits, and openings
  • Shop primer and finish coatings
